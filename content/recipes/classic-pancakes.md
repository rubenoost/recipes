title: "Classic Pancakes"
date: 2024-12-28

description: "Fluffy, golden pancakes perfect for a weekend breakfast"
image: ""
servings: 4
prep_time: "10 minutes"
cook_time: "15 minutes"
categories: ["breakfast"]
tags: ["quick", "vegetarian", "kid-friendly"]
ingredients:
  - item: "all-purpose flour"
    amount: "1½ cups"
  - item: "milk"
    amount: "1¼ cups"
  - item: "egg"
    amount: "1 large"
  - item: "melted butter"
    amount: "3 tbsp"
  - item: "sugar"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - item: "baking powder"
    amount: "1½ tsp"
  - item: "salt"
    amount: "½ tsp"
instructions:
  - step: "In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt."
  - step: "In a separate bowl, beat the egg, then add milk and melted butter. Mix well."
  - step: "Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Don't overmix - a few lumps are fine."
  - step: "Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease if needed."
  - step: "Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form on surface and edges look set, about 2-3 minutes."
  - step: "Flip and cook another 1-2 minutes until golden brown."
  - step: "Serve warm with maple syrup, fresh berries, or your favorite toppings."
---

For extra fluffy pancakes, let the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking. You can also separate the egg and fold in the beaten egg white at the end for an even lighter texture.
